About the Artist

Casey Hilton

My work explores the spaces between memory and identity, examining how personal history shapes the way we understand ourselves and others. Through painting and mixed-media practices, I investigate themes of family, grief, mental health, relationships, and the stories we inherit — whether willingly or not.

I am drawn to moments of contradiction: strength and vulnerability, permanence and impermanence, connection and isolation.
